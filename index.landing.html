FAQ

Common questions

What is Floworax? +
Floworax is the parent company behind Flowora — a suite of business tools built for Nigerian small businesses. Our first product, Flowora Pay, helps shop owners and traders record sales, accept payments, and track revenue, even without internet.
Does it work without internet? +
Yes. Flowora Pay is built offline-first. You can record sales, view reports, and manage your store with no internet connection. Everything syncs automatically to the cloud when you come back online.
How do payments work? +
Flowora uses Paystack to process payments. Your customers can pay by card, bank transfer, or USSD. Payments are verified automatically and settled directly to your bank account by Paystack — we never hold your money.
Is my data safe? +
Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt — we never store plain text passwords. Your sales data is backed up to secure cloud servers and you can export it anytime.
Can I use it on my phone? +
Yes. Flowora is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on your Android or iPhone home screen and it works like a native app. No app store required.
When is Flowora Bookings coming? +
Flowora Bookings is in development and will be available to Pro and Business plan users first. Join our waitlist below to be notified when it launches.
